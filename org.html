About PUBG Mobile

PUBG Mobile is a free-to-play battle royale game where up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing. Players can choose to enter the match solo, duo, or with a small team of up to four people. Each match starts with players parachuting onto one of the maps. The map area decreases in size over time, directing surviving players into tighter areas to force encounters. The last player or team standing wins the match.
