Article I — Purpose & Scope

This Agreement is entered into between Bari Plux (hereinafter "Server Provider") and DevilJin (hereinafter "Manager"), collectively referred to as "the Parties," for the purpose of establishing a Discord-based community service dedicated to assisting users with Steam games.

The core objective of this collaboration is to operate a Discord server that provides help, guidance, troubleshooting, and community support for Steam game-related inquiries to its members.

Article II — Roles & Responsibilities

2.1 — Bari Plux (Server Provider)

Bari Plux agrees to provide and maintain access to the Discord server infrastructure for the duration of this Agreement. The Server Provider's sole obligation is the technical availability of the server environment. Bari Plux bears no responsibility for any content, actions, events, or incidents occurring within the server.

2.2 — DevilJin (Manager)

DevilJin assumes full and exclusive managerial authority over the Discord server and takes on all responsibilities, including but not limited to:

  1. Day-to-day management, moderation, and operation of the Discord server.
  2. Recruiting, managing, and overseeing any staff or moderators.
  3. Organizing and executing all events, giveaways, or community activities.
  4. Ensuring all server content complies with Discord's Terms of Service and applicable laws.
  5. Handling all member disputes, complaints, or misconduct.
  6. Managing all announcements, channels, roles, and bot integrations.
  7. Responding to and resolving any issues raised by community members.

Article III — Liability & Accountability

DevilJin shall bear sole and complete liability for all actions, events, incidents, and consequences arising within or from the operation of the Discord server.

This includes, without limitation:

Any disputes, damages, or claims arising from user interactions within the server.
Any violations of Discord's Terms of Service or community guidelines.
Any financial obligations resulting from server activities, events, or promotions.
Any legal liability arising from content posted or activities conducted in the server.

Bari Plux is fully indemnified and held harmless from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from the server's operation.

Article IV — Steam Gaming Assistance Service

The primary service provided through this server shall include:

  1. Technical assistance and troubleshooting for Steam games.
  2. Game recommendations, tips, guides, and walkthroughs.
  3. Community discussion and support channels dedicated to specific games.
  4. Facilitation of cooperative play, game sessions, and community events.

DevilJin is responsible for ensuring the quality, accuracy, and appropriateness of all assistance and content provided.

Article V — Term & Termination

This Agreement shall commence on the effective date above and shall remain in force indefinitely unless terminated by either Party.

Either Party may terminate this Agreement by providing 14 days written notice to the other Party via Discord direct message or another agreed-upon channel.
In the event of termination, DevilJin remains liable for all obligations and incidents that occurred prior to the termination date.
Upon termination, Bari Plux reserves the right to revoke server access immediately if deemed necessary.

Article VI — Amendments

Any amendments or modifications to this Agreement must be mutually agreed upon in writing by both Parties. Verbal agreements shall not be considered binding modifications to this contract.
