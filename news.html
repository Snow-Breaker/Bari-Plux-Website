PMGC 2025 Teams

The PUBG Mobile Global Championship 2025 features 48 of the best teams from around the world, divided into 3 regional groups:

Group A (Americas & Europe): Team Queso, Faze Clan, Natus Vincere, Knights, A7 Esports, and 11 more teams

Group B (Asia): Nova Esports, 4 Angry Men, Vampire Esports, D'Xavier, Bigetron RA, and 10 more teams

Group C (South Asia & MENA): Team Soul, GodLike Esports, Skylightz Gaming, Trained to Kill, i8 Esports, and 9 more teams

The tournament will run for 5 weeks with Group Stages, Quarter Finals, Semi Finals, and the Grand Finals in Istanbul.
